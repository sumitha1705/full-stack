About Us

Welcome to our Book Management System — a platform designed to make managing books easy, efficient, and enjoyable. Whether you are a librarian, student, teacher, or just a book lover, our goal is to simplify how you organize, track, and access books.

Our platform provides tools to:

  • Add, remove, and update book records.
  • Track borrowed and returned books.
  • Search and categorize books by title, author, or genre.
  • Enable user logins for personalized management.

Built using modern web technologies like FastAPI and PostgreSQL, our system ensures speed, reliability, and scalability. Our mission is to promote reading and knowledge-sharing through an intuitive and powerful digital solution.

Thank you for choosing us as your book management partner. We’re constantly improving, so feel free to reach out with feedback or suggestions.
